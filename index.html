Burst

Burst is a workshop molded especially for creatives. The workshop touches base on basic Videography and Photography skills. Not only can you learn new skills, but you may also network with the creative community around you!

Learn hands-on how to work with today's latest 4k camera equipment. From Cameras and lighting to gimbals and stabilizers.

Location of the event will take place in NYC studio. The location of the studio will be announced the day before the event, via email.

Utilize our studio space and experience what it's like to be in a professional photography environment. (also with latest camera equipment).
